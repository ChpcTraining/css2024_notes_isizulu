# Siyakwamukela

Siyakwamukela kwi-Coding Summer School 2024.

Sinezikhungo zocwaningo ezingu-30 eNingizimu Afrika ezizoba yingxenye yalesi sikole kanjalo namanyuvesi aqhamuka emazweni angomakhelwane.
Sinabafundi abangaphezu kuka-800 abaneziqu nabaqeqeshiwe abazobamba iqhaza.

I-Coding Summer School (CSS) ihlelwe ngokuhlanganyela yi-Centre for High Performance Computing (CHPC) ye-CSIR kanye ne-National Institute for Theoretical Computational Science (NITheCS). Lo mzamo ohlanganyelwe ugqugquzelwe ngokukhethekile iphepha lomqondo woMnyango wezeSayensi nokuQala kabusha waseNingizimu Afrika (i-DSI) elihlola ukuthi kungenzeka yini ukunweba ububanzi be-NITheP ibe isikhungo/isikhungo sikazwelonke sesayensi yetiyori kanye nekhompyutha. Lona unyaka we-14 umcimbi walolu hlobo wenzeke nge-CHPC kanye nonyaka wesi-6 ngokubambisana ne-NITheCS.

Sonke sijabule ngokuthi ujoyine lesi sikole sasehlobo esiyingqopha-mlando!

Kungani Ufunda Ukubhala Ikhodi Kwabaphothulile Ezindaweni ze-STEM

Amakhono okubhala amakhodi abalulekile emikhakheni eminingi ye-STEM, okuhlanganisa isayensi yedatha, i-bioinformatics, isayensi yekhompyutha, nobunjiniyela. Ukuba nolwazi lokubhala amakhodi kukuvumela ukuthi uhlaziye futhi ulawule amasethi amakhulu edatha, uthuthukise amamodeli nezifaniso, futhi wenze imisebenzi ephindaphindwayo ngokuzenzakalelayo.

Ukubhala amakhodi kungakusiza futhi ukuthi uxhumane nocwaningo lwakho ngempumelelo kakhulu. Isibonelo, ungakha ukuboniswa okusebenzisanayo, izinhlelo zokusebenza zewebhu, nezifaniso ukuze udlulise okutholile kuzithameli eziningi.

Ukubhala amakhodi kungakusiza ukuthi uthuthukise amakhono okuxazulula izinkinga. Ukubhala amakhodi kudinga ukucabanga okunengqondo kanye nekhono lokubola inkinga ibe izingxenye ezincane, ezilawulekayo. Lawa makhono adluliselwa kwezinye izindawo zocwaningo lwakho.

Ukubhala amakhodi kungakusiza ukuthi usebenzisane ngempumelelo. Amaphrojekthi amaningi ocwaningo abandakanya ukusebenzisana, futhi ukuba namakhono okubhala amakhodi kungakusiza ukuthi usebenze ngokuphumelelayo nabanye abacwaningi. Ukwengeza, ukubhala ngekhodi kuwulimi lomhlaba wonke olungakusiza ukuthi uxhumane nabacwaningi abavela ezizindeni ezihlukene.

Okokugcina, amakhono okubhala amakhodi aziswa kakhulu embonini, ngakho ukufunda ukubhala ikhodi kungavula amathuba amasha emisebenzi ngemva kokuphothula.

Ulimi lokubhala amakhodi noma lokuhlela esizolusebenzisa kulesi sikole iPython. Kubhekwa kabanzi njengolunye lwezilimi ezinhle kakhulu zokuhlela kulabo abasemkhakheni we-STEM (isayensi, ubuchwepheshe, ubunjiniyela, kanye nezibalo) ngenxa yobulula bawo, ukwenza izinto ezihlukahlukene, nokuthandwa kulo mkhakha. Inomphakathi omkhulu futhi osebenzayo, ohlinzeka ingcebo yemitapo yolwazi nezinsiza zokwenza ikhompuyutha yesayensi, ukuhlaziya idatha, ukufunda ngomshini, nokubona ngeso lengqondo. Ukwengeza, ukufundeka kwePython nokusebenziseka kalula kuyenza ibe yisinqumo esihle kubafundi abathola iziqu abangaba basha ezinhlelweni noma abafuna ukwenza i-prototype ngokushesha kanye nemibono yokuhlola. Sizokwenza okuningi kwalokhu lapho siqala izifundo ezihlelekile.

Yize iPython kuwulimi oluyisisekelo lokuhlela ozolufunda, kukhona "olunye" ozolufunda olubizwa nge-Bash. Okungelona ngokobuchwepheshe ulimi lokuhlela kodwa igobolondo lomugqa womyalo. Kodwa-ke, kusivumela ukuthi sihlanganyele nesistimu yokusebenza futhi senze imisebenzi ngokuzenzakalelayo futhi senze ukuphathwa kwefayela ngempumelelo. Kuyasiza futhi ukwazi njengoba kudingekile ukusebenzisa amasistimu we-HPC (High Performance Computing). Okuningi ngalokhu kamuva.
